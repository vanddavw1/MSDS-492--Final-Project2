OPEC launches 2018 Annual Statistical Bulletin

07 Jun 2018 | Vienna, Austria

The OPEC Secretariat has announced the release of the 53rd edition of the Annual Statistical Bulletin (ASB). This years ASB provides updated statistical data on the oil and natural gas activities of each of OPECs 14 Member Countries: Algeria, Angola, Ecuador, Equatorial Guinea, Gabon, Islamic Republic of Iran, Iraq, Kuwait, Libya, Nigeria, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates and Venezuela. As in previous years, it also contains valuable industry data for various countries around the world, organized mainly by geographic region, and covers the worlds major economic areas.
